Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Business name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
Payment Information:
  • Payment card details (processed securely through PayPal/Stripe)
  • Billing address
  • Transaction history
Usage Information:
  • Leads viewed and accessed
  • Search queries and filters used
  • Credits purchased and spent
  • Login activity and timestamps
Technical Information:
  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Cookies and tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain the lead generation service
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account
  • Billing: To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Communication: To send service updates, lead alerts, and promotional emails
  • Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our service
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We Do NOT Sell Your Personal Information
We May Share Information With:
  • Payment Processors: PayPal, Stripe, and other payment providers to process transactions
  • Email Service Providers: To send notifications and updates
  • Analytics Services: To understand usage patterns (anonymized data)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted passwords using industry-standard hashing
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your account
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@vacontracts.com

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data retained for 12 months after last login
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Upon Request: We will delete your data within 30 days of a deletion request (except where legally required to retain)

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

  • Maintaining your login session
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing site usage
  • Improving user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of the Service.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Service contains links to government websites and commercial business websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Sending an email to your registered address
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

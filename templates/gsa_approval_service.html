Required Documents for GSA Approval

Important Disclaimer: The following documents are required by GSA. Please ensure all information is accurate, complete, and up-to-date. Incomplete applications cause significant delays (3-6 additional months).
Company Information
  • SAM Registration: Active SAM.gov registration (must be current)
  • DUNS Number: Dun & Bradstreet DUNS number
  • Tax ID (EIN): Federal Employer Identification Number
  • Business License: Current state/local business license
  • Articles of Incorporation: Or equivalent formation documents
  • W-9 Form: Completed and signed
Financial Documents
  • Financial Statements: Last 3 years (audited if available)
  • Tax Returns: Business tax returns (last 3 years)
  • Credit Report: Business credit report (D&B or similar)
  • Banking Reference: Letter from your business bank
Experience & Qualifications
  • Past Performance: 3+ references from recent contracts
  • Contract History: List of major contracts (last 3 years)
  • Company Resume: Capabilities statement
  • Certifications: Industry certifications (CIMS, ISSA, etc.)
  • Insurance: Proof of liability, workers' comp insurance
Pricing & Services
  • Service Catalog: List of services you'll offer
  • Price List: Commercial pricing for all services
  • Discount Structure: Proposed GSA pricing/discounts
  • Labor Categories: Job positions and hourly rates

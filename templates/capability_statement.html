What is a Capability Statement?

A capability statement is a one-page document that showcases your company's qualifications, past performance, and core competencies. It's essential for government contracting and helps you stand out when networking or responding to RFPs.

Tips for Success

DO:
  • Keep it to ONE page
  • Use your company's branding/colors
  • Include specific dollar amounts and dates
  • Update quarterly with new projects
  • Use professional photo quality
  • Print on quality paper for in-person meetings
  • Have both PDF and printed versions ready
DON'T:
  • Exceed one page (use smaller font if needed)
  • Use generic stock photos
  • Include outdated information
  • Forget to update SAM.gov registration
  • Use too much industry jargon
  • Leave out contact information
  • Forget to proofread carefully

When to Use Your Capability Statement

Networking Events

Bring printed copies to industry conferences and meetups

Cold Outreach

Include with introduction emails to procurement officers

RFP Responses

Attach as supporting documentation in proposals

Teaming Agreements

Share with potential partners for joint ventures

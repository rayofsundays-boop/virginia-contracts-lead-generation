What are "Quick Wins"?
Quick Win Contracts Include:
  • Short deadlines (2-4 weeks)
  • Smaller dollar amounts ($10K-$150K)
  • Standing orders (ongoing relationships)
  • Emergency procurements (fast turnaround)
  • Small business set-asides
  • Pilot programs (foot in the door)
Perfect For:
  • New suppliers breaking into government market
  • Companies with inventory ready to ship
  • Local/regional distributors
  • Businesses seeking faster cash flow
  • Building track record for larger contracts
Pro Tip: Quick wins often lead to multi-year contracts! Many agencies start with trial periods before committing to larger purchases.

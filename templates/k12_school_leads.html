Tips for Winning K-12 School Contracts
  • Background Checks: Rigorous background screening required for all staff working in schools
  • Academic Calendar Flexibility: Must accommodate school schedules, breaks, and summer deep cleaning
  • Child-Safe Products: Use EPA-approved, low-toxicity cleaning products safe for children
  • Food Service Areas: Experience with cafeteria and kitchen sanitation is critical
  • Multi-Site Management: Large districts require coordinated services across many locations
  • Emergency Response: 24/7 availability for urgent cleaning needs (illness outbreaks, accidents)
  • Community Engagement: Schools value local businesses and community involvement
  • Performance-Based Renewals: Consistent quality leads to multi-year contract extensions

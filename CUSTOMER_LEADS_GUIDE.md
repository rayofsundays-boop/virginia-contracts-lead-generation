## üí° How to Use

### 1. **Browse All Leads**
   - Scroll through the complete list of 106+ opportunities
   - Each card shows: title, agency, location, value, deadline, and services needed

### 2. **Filter by Region**
   - Use location filter to focus on specific cities
   - Options: Hampton, Norfolk, Virginia Beach, Richmond, Northern Virginia, etc.

### 3. **Filter by Lead Type**
   - Government contracts for traditional procurement
   - Commercial opportunities for direct business-to-business deals
   - Client requests for immediate service opportunities

### 4. **Sort by Priority**
   - High-value contracts first (e.g., Fairfax County $18M-$28M)
   - Urgent deadlines (ending soon)
   - Newest opportunities (recently posted)

### 5. **View Details**
   - Click on any lead to see full description
   - Requirements and specifications
   - Application URLs and contact information
   - NAICS codes for federal contracts

### 6. **Save Leads**
   - Save promising opportunities to your personal repository
   - Track application status
   - Set reminders for deadlines

### 7. **Generate Proposals**
   - Use built-in proposal generator for saved leads
   - Customize templates for different contract types
   - Export or email proposals directly
